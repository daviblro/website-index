About

WOS Nerds is all about digging into data, running tests, and figuring out the best strategies. We'd like to minimise any guides/advice that's not based on some solid/decent proof.

Our focus is simple: find out what works, why it works, and how it can be improved. We love data, calculators, spreadsheets and using it to win!

Think our Website is very... unpolished/unfinished? It is. Everything is mostly in draft mode at the moment. Come contribute and help us though!

All data is free to copy and use. See https://github.com/wosnerdwarriors for source code
